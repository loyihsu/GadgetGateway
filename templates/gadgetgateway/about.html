GadgetGateway is a social news and opinions website focusing on technology products. It features user posted threads about laptops, smartphones, wearables, headphones and gadgets. Threads are ranked based on popularity of views and upvotes, as vote by other registered members. Members can submit their own comments and reviews for each product as well as searching for what they looking for based on the implemented search engine.
